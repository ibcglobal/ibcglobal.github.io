Visit visa consultancy support

Visit visa applications can involve identity documents, financial evidence, accommodation details, travel plans and evidence of the purpose of travel. Requirements vary by country and may change.

Our general guidance may include

  • Application requirement review
  • Document checklist guidance
  • Travel itinerary planning support
  • Hotel and flight reservation coordination
  • General invitation and supporting-document guidance
  • Application-readiness review
No visa guarantee: IndusBaltic Consultancy cannot influence embassy decisions and does not guarantee visa approval.

Your responsibilities

Applicants are responsible for providing truthful, complete and authentic information. False documents or misleading information may lead to refusal and other consequences.

Professional boundaries

Our service is general consultancy and preparation support. It is not legal representation or immigration legal advice.
