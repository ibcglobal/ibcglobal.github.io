Career guidance with an international perspective

Career planning can involve education choices, skills development, professional interests, location, language, finances and family circumstances. Our role is to help you organise these factors and make a better-informed decision.

Career counselling may cover

  • Education and employment background review
  • Career interest and direction discussion
  • Skills-gap identification
  • Study options connected to career goals
  • International destination considerations
  • CV and professional profile guidance
  • General interview preparation guidance

Support for students

Students can use career counselling to understand how subject choices, qualifications and international study may connect with future professional goals.

Support for professionals

Professionals may use the service to review further education, skills development, international mobility and possible career changes.

Please note: Career counselling does not guarantee employment, work permits, professional licensing or a particular salary.
