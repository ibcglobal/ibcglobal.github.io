Our identity

Why the name IndusBaltic?

“Indus” represents our Pakistani roots and the historic Indus region. “Baltic” reflects our European connection through Poland and the wider Baltic and European region.

Together, the name represents connection, mobility, education and the movement of people and ideas between regions.

Our purpose

Our purpose is to provide organised, transparent and realistic guidance for education, career, visa and travel decisions.
