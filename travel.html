Before booking

Travellers should confirm passport validity, visa requirements, baggage rules, transit requirements, cancellation conditions and insurance needs before finalising a booking.

Information we may request

  • Traveller names exactly as shown on passports
  • Preferred departure and arrival cities
  • Flexible or fixed travel dates
  • Passenger ages
  • Baggage and transit preferences
  • Preferred hotel category and budget
Booking notice: Prices and availability can change before a booking is confirmed. Airline and hotel rules apply to all reservations.
