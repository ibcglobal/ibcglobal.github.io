Education counselling services

Choosing an international institution requires more than selecting a country. Students should also consider programme content, entry requirements, language expectations, financial planning, location and long-term career relevance.

IndusBaltic Consultancy helps students organise these questions and compare available options before taking the next step.

Our guidance may include

  • Initial student profile and goal review
  • Country and destination comparison
  • Course and programme selection guidance
  • Institution research and general entry requirement review
  • Application document preparation guidance
  • Personal statement and supporting document guidance
  • General pre-departure and travel planning support
Important: Admission decisions are made only by the relevant educational institution. IndusBaltic does not guarantee admission, scholarships or acceptance.

Destination focus

We provide guidance relating to educational institutions in Poland, wider Europe, the United Kingdom, the United States and the Middle East. Recommendations are based on the information supplied by the student and publicly available institution requirements.

Who this service is for

  • School and college graduates exploring international study
  • University graduates considering postgraduate education
  • Professionals seeking further education or specialist study
  • Parents seeking structured guidance for a student
