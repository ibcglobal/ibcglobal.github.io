International education counselling

Choosing where and what to study involves more than selecting a country. Course content, entry requirements, language, finances, location and career plans all matter.

We help students and families organise these questions before making important decisions.

Our guidance may include

  • Student profile and goals review
  • Country and destination comparison
  • Course and institution research
  • General entry requirement review
  • Application document guidance
  • Personal statement preparation guidance
  • General pre-departure planning

Destination focus

Our current destination focus includes Poland, wider Europe, the United Kingdom, the United States and the Middle East.
